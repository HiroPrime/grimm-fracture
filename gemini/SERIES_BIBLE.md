# Grimm Fracture — Series Bible (CANON)

**Format:** Web comic series  
**Creator:** BasicHiro  
**Label:** Core Node Original  
**Status:** Leap 1 in development / marketing live  

---

## Logline

When the Sacred Book of Fables is obliterated into volatile pages, Red and Alice Leap across fractured eras as Echoes — scavenging weapons in sixty seconds, poisoned by chronal fatigue — racing the Syndicate to reclaim Keystone Pages before reality collapses.

---

## Core premise (non-negotiable)

1. **The Book was destroyed**, not merely torn. Thousands of volatile pages now scatter across eras.
2. **The enemy weaponized the fragments.** Dual Proxies command Constructs and Echoes as Phalanx Fodder / assassins.
3. **Travelers (heroes) recover glowing stacks of parchment** — the weight of their reality.
4. **The chronal stream destroys physical matter.** They arrive empty-handed every Leap.
5. **Sixty seconds to scavenge a weapon** after landing.
6. **Chronal fatigue** poisons their blood — visible exhaustion, sickness, urgency.
7. **Magic is stripped.** They adapt using era-appropriate Echo identities and scavenged tech/chemistry.
8. **They keep Leaping** — world to world, era to era — forced to learn new rules each time.

## Tone & themes

- Exhaustion as heroism  
- Fairy tales as industrial horror  
- Adaptation under constraint  
- Found family under fire (Red ↔ Alice tension + trust)  
- Authority as armored absurdity (Syndicate)  
- Memory / identity fracture (Echoes who “are” and “aren’t” the myth)

**Not:** whimsical Wonderland pastiche, clean superhero poses, soft romance-first framing.

## Leap structure (production template)

Every Leap file should define:

1. **Era + grid name**  
2. **Visual thesis** (one sentence)  
3. **Keystone Page objective**  
4. **Local rules / twist**  
5. **Echo remaps** for Red & Alice (costume + skill echo)  
6. **Enemy kit** (2–3 unit types max for clarity)  
7. **3-act beat map**  
8. **Page budget** (target)  
9. **Cold open hook** for web serialization  

---

## Serialization rules (web comic)

- **Hook early:** page 1 must show danger or Leap consequence within 2 panels.  
- **Cliff micro-hooks:** end pages on a question, threat, or visual punch — not a soft fade.  
- **Readability first:** max 6–7 panels/page unless splash.  
- **Character silhouette test:** Red = axe + red duster; Alice = apron + vials + bow; readable at thumbnail size.  
- **Batch thinking:** write 4–8 pages ahead of art when possible.

---

## Continuity locks

- Red and Alice land **together** unless a Leap file explicitly splits them.  
- They cannot bring weapons across Leaps.  
- Chronal fatigue worsens with repeated Leaping — show progression across arcs.  
- Keystone Pages glow; they feel “heavy” with narrative weight.  
- Syndicate units are not redeemable allies in Leap 1.
