## Visual thesis

Victorian London architecture choked by aggressive mutated flora; streets slick with smog and spilled tonics while the Syndicate harvests the ruins.

## Local rules / twist

- Flora behaves like a second enemy — grows overnight, seals alleys, feeds on tonic runoff.  
- Alchemy / botanical chemistry works; classic magic does not.  
- Narrow alleys favor Ironclad shield walls; rooftops favor Stalkers.  
- Travelers arrive empty-handed → 60-second scavenge.
